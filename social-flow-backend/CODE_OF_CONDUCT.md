# 🤝 **Code of Conduct**

## **🌟 Our Pledge**

We, as contributors, maintainers, and participants in the **Social Flow Backend** community, pledge to make participation in our project and community a **harassment-free experience** for everyone, regardless of:

- 👥 Age, body size, visible or invisible disability
- 🌍 Ethnicity, nationality, race, or immigration status  
- 🏳️‍🌈 Gender identity and expression, sexual orientation
- 📚 Level of experience, education, or technical expertise
- 🧠 Neurodiversity and mental health status
- 💰 Personal appearance, clothing, or socio-economic status
- 🙏 Religion, political views, or other opinion

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community**.

---

## ✅ **Our Standards**

### **🌟 Examples of Positive Behavior**

**We encourage and celebrate:**

- 💬 **Inclusive Communication**: Using welcoming, respectful, and inclusive language
- 🤝 **Respectful Interaction**: Being respectful of differing viewpoints, experiences, and perspectives
- 💪 **Constructive Feedback**: Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism and feedback
- 🎯 **Community Focus**: Focusing on what is best for the overall community and project
- ❤️ **Empathy**: Showing empathy, kindness, and understanding towards other community members
- 🙏 **Accountability**: Taking responsibility for our mistakes, apologizing, and learning from experiences
- 📚 **Learning**: Helping others learn and being open to learning from others
- 🎉 **Recognition**: Acknowledging and appreciating others' contributions and efforts
- 🔍 **Transparency**: Being honest and transparent in communications and decision-making
- 🌱 **Growth**: Supporting personal and professional growth of community members

### **❌ Examples of Unacceptable Behavior**

**The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:**

- 🚫 **Harassment**: Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, race, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics
- 💬 **Inappropriate Content**: Sexual language, imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- 🗣️ **Disruptive Behavior**: Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- 📍 **Stalking**: Following, monitoring, or tracking someone without their consent
- 📢 **Privacy Violations**: Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without explicit permission
- 🎭 **Impersonation**: Pretending to be someone else or misrepresenting affiliation
- 📧 **Spam**: Unsolicited promotional content, repeated off-topic messages, or flooding channels
- 🔗 **Malicious Links**: Sharing harmful software, phishing links, or malicious content
- ⚖️ **Legal Violations**: Encouraging or engaging in illegal activities
- 💼 **Professional Misconduct**: Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## 🛡️ **Enforcement Responsibilities**

### **👮 Project Maintainers**

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- 📋 **Clarifying Standards**: Clearly communicating and explaining the standards of acceptable behavior
- ⚖️ **Fair Enforcement**: Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to violations
- 🔧 **Content Moderation**: Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- 🚫 **Access Control**: Temporarily or permanently banning contributors for inappropriate behavior
- 🔍 **Investigation**: Thoroughly investigating reports of misconduct
- 📝 **Documentation**: Maintaining records of enforcement actions (while respecting privacy)

### **🎯 Enforcement Actions**

Depending on the severity and context, enforcement actions may include:

1. **📝 Informal Warning**: Private conversation about the behavior and expectations
2. **⚠️ Formal Warning**: Written warning with consequences for continued violations
3. **⏸️ Temporary Suspension**: Temporary removal from community spaces and interactions
4. **🚫 Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from all community spaces and interactions
5. **📞 Escalation**: Involving law enforcement for illegal activities or serious threats

## 🚨 **Reporting Guidelines**

### **📞 How to Report**

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it promptly:

#### **📧 Primary Contact**
- **Email**: conduct@socialflow.com
- **Response Time**: 24-48 hours
- **Confidentiality**: All reports are treated confidentially

#### **🔒 Anonymous Reporting**
- **Anonymous Form**: [Report Form](https://forms.socialflow.com/conduct)
- **Secure**: No personal information required
- **Follow-up**: Optional contact information for updates

#### **🆘 Emergency Contact**
For immediate safety concerns:
- **Emergency Email**: emergency@socialflow.com
- **Phone**: Available for critical situations

### **📋 What to Include in Your Report**

To help us address the situation effectively, please include:

- 📅 **Date and time** of the incident
- 📍 **Location** (which platform, channel, or event)
- 👥 **People involved** (names, usernames, handles)
- 📝 **Detailed description** of what happened
- 📸 **Evidence** (screenshots, logs, witnesses)
- 🎯 **Impact** on you or the community
- 💡 **Desired outcome** or resolution

### **🔍 Investigation Process**

1. **📨 Acknowledgment**: We acknowledge receipt within 24-48 hours
2. **🔍 Investigation**: Thorough review of the incident and evidence
3. **👥 Interviews**: Speaking with involved parties and witnesses
4. **⚖️ Decision**: Determining appropriate action based on findings
5. **📋 Communication**: Informing the reporter of the outcome
6. **📊 Follow-up**: Monitoring for retaliation or repeat incidents

---

## 🛡️ **Protection Against Retaliation**

We strictly prohibit retaliation against anyone who:
- 📢 Reports violations in good faith
- 👥 Participates in an investigation
- 🤝 Supports someone who has reported violations

**Retaliation is considered a serious violation** and will result in appropriate enforcement action.
